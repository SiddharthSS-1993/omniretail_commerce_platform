# Product Requirements: OmniRetail Commerce Platform

## 1. Problem Statement

Retail organizations generate data across multiple business domains, including customers, products, stores, suppliers, inventory, pricing, promotions, and sales transactions. This data is often distributed across operational systems, making it difficult to obtain a unified analytical view of business performance.

The OmniRetail Commerce Platform aims to build a scalable retail data platform that supports analytics, reporting, customer intelligence, inventory optimization, and future machine learning use cases.

---

## 2. Goals

- Ingest retail data from multiple business domains.
- Preserve raw source data.
- Build cleaned and integrated datasets.
- Produce business-ready analytical datasets.
- Support future forecasting and customer analytics.

---

## 3. Users

- Retail Operations Manager
- Store Manager
- Merchandising Manager
- Inventory Planner
- Pricing Analyst
- Data Analyst
- Data Engineer
- Data Scientist

---

## 4. Functional Requirements

The platform shall:

- Ingest customer data.
- Ingest product data.
- Ingest supplier data.
- Ingest store data.
- Ingest order data.
- Ingest order item data.
- Ingest inventory data.
- Ingest promotion data.
- Preserve raw source data.
- Produce cleaned analytical datasets.
- Support retail KPI reporting.

---

## 5. Non-Functional Requirements

The platform should:

- Be modular.
- Be reproducible locally.
- Support scalable synthetic data generation.
- Support automated testing.
- Maintain lightweight source control.
- Be extensible for future cloud deployment.

---

## 6. Out of Scope

The initial version does not include:

- Payment gateway integration.
- Live point-of-sale systems.
- Real-time inventory synchronization.
- Cloud deployment.
- Customer-facing applications.
